How it works
Answered questions sit at the top as small clickable chips. A subtle pencil icon (✎) hints they're editable. Click one → it expands inline to a small edit field right in the chip row, current value pre-filled. Hit Save (or press Enter) and it collapses back. No separate page, no "go back" button.
